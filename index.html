Hosted by Jon Orozco

Not a checklist tour. A hosted way into the city.

This is a small-group food experience built around the way Mexico City actually opens itself up: one neighborhood, one table, one market stall, one late-night bite, and one beautiful surprise at a time.

Raised between Mexican agricultural roots and migrant farm life in Washington state, Jon brings a personal connection to the food, labor, hospitality, and regional traditions behind every meal. Expect deep hospitality, honest recommendations, enough structure to relax, and enough surprise to make the trip feel alive.
